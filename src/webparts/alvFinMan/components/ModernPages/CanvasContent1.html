IMPLEMENTATION OF IPU FEE in all months 2022
The process for reporting and recording the IPU Fee:

  1. Reporting in P&L on OneStream account 421000IC (HFM account 43070), with intercompany specification should be used for IPU Fee. No reconciliation on the Balance Sheet, but you should report the IPU Fee on account 711440OPE.
  2. CHBAGO (CHZ) is the counterparty of the IPU Fee for all companies.
  3. During January to November, we have made accruals for the IPU Fee (announced separately).
  4. By mid-December, CHBAGO (CHZ) issues invoices for the amount of the full year IPU Fee, based on November YTD actual IPU Spend, plus an estimate of December spend.
  5. A true up process will occur during Q1 2023, for differences between the estimated 2022 IPU spend for the invoice and the actual IPU spend.
  6. Updated IPU Fee accrual amounts will be communicated by CHBAGO (CHZ) in JUN for second half of the year.

 

The information to accrue for the IPU Fee is distributed by Doug Thompson at Corporate tax.
 

LEASING STANDARD AND KLT REPORTING
We want to remind you that the silent period after each reporting deadline is unchanged, meaning that there should be no changes in KLT three working days after every AC reporting deadline, since Group financial control needs these days to analyze and validate data in KLT. Therefore, please do not perform any changes or modifications in KLT during these days. 

If you have any technical questions regarding KLT please contact KPMG helpdesk ( us-advspectrum@KPMG.com ) and if you have any other questions regarding the lease reporting reach out to your division’s KLT super user.

CURRENCY EXPOSURE REPORTING
The currency exposure should preferably be reported in connection to the actual reporting deadline, and no later than maximum 5 days after.
Please respect the deadline as the process and the outcome of the figures involve many different counterparties in the group.
